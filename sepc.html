MoU Active (2025-2030)

Strategic Partnership Overview

The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) has maintained a resilient partnership with the Services Export Promotion Council (SEPC) since June 2020. This collaboration serves as a cornerstone for promoting Indian professional excellence on the global stage.

On July 1, 2025, the MoU was renewed for an additional five years, cementing a long-term commitment to enhancing international mobility and services export growth.

About Global ICAI

The Global Trade & Services Committee of ICAI is committed to strengthening India’s presence in global services trade by promoting international collaboration, enhancing professional opportunities, and positioning accounting and finance as key export services in the evolving global economy. The Committee works closely with government bodies, industry stakeholders, and international institutions to facilitate trade integration, policy engagement, and global professional mobility.
