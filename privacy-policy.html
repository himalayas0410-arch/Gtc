Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) – Global Trade & Services Committee collects, uses, and protects user information.

Last updated: April 2026
Information We Collect How We Use Information Cookies Data Protection Third-Party Sharing User Rights Contact
1

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our website:

  • Personal information such as name, email address, and phone number provided through forms or registrations
  • Usage data including pages visited, time spent on the site, browser type, and interaction patterns
2

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and deliver our services, programs, and professional resources effectively
  • To communicate updates, announcements, and relevant information regarding committee activities
  • To improve website functionality, user experience, and content relevance
3

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our site and allow us to improve functionality and performance.

You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, although this may affect certain features of the website.

4

Data Protection

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to safeguard your personal information:

  • All information is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security protocols
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel only
  • We regularly review and update our security practices to prevent unauthorised access, disclosure, or misuse
5

Third-Party Sharing

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data:

  • Your personal data is not sold, rented, or traded to any third party for commercial purposes
  • Information may only be shared when required by law, regulation, or legal proceedings
6

User Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to applicable legal obligations

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

7

Contact Information

For any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at:

About Global ICAI

The Global Trade & Services Committee of ICAI is committed to strengthening India’s presence in global services trade by promoting international collaboration, enhancing professional opportunities, and positioning accounting and finance as key export services in the evolving global economy. The Committee works closely with government bodies, industry stakeholders, and international institutions to facilitate trade integration, policy engagement, and global professional mobility.
